Regenerative braking systems: Regenerative braking systems (RBSs) are a type of kinetic energy recovery system that transfers the kinetic energy of an object in motion into potential or stored energy to slow the vehicle down, and as a result increases fuel efficiency. Each type of RBS utilizes a different energy conversion or storage method, giving varying efficiency and applications for each type.
