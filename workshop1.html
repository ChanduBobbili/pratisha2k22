CNC: Modern CNC machine tools use a CNC program to sequentially remove raw materials from the workpiece, which is programmed to guide the machining process. ... Manufacturers use software and machines including milling machines, lathes, and to improve their performance. CNC machining provides the benefit of every tool you can imagine in the hands of an expert craftsman. If you're looking to make just about anything, the right CNC technician will be able to program it for you and make as many copies as you like!
