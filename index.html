ETABS

Workshop on 7th & 8th April
ETABS is an engineering software product that caters to multi-storey building analysis & design .Modelling tools & templates,code based load prescriptions,analysis methods & solution techniques . all coordinate with grid like geometry unique to this class of structure basic or advanced systems under static or dynamic conditions can be evaluated using ETABS.

ETABS software is used for the analysis of concrete shear walls and concrete moment frames. It is highly acclaimed for static and dynamic analysis of multi-storey frame and shear wall buildings. ETABS will be teached by the eminent personalities from IIT BOMBAY with IIT certification.

Registration fee: ₹800
